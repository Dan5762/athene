Athene Club is a London-based running and hiking club exclusively for women. Founded in 2010, our mission is to create a supportive and empowering community where women can explore their passion for outdoor activities.

Whether you're a seasoned marathon runner or just starting your fitness journey, Athene Club offers a variety of events and training programs to suit all levels. Join us for weekly group runs, weekend hiking trips, and social gatherings.

We believe in the power of female camaraderie and the importance of physical well-being. At Athene Club, you'll find a welcoming environment where you can challenge yourself, make new friends, and discover the beauty of the outdoors.
